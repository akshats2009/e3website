Who We Are

The e3 initiative is a student-led initiative committed to advancing understanding at the critical intersection of equity, economics, and ethnicity. We aim to amplify minority voices, highlight overlooked local and historical experiences, and bridge academic research with community storytelling.

What We Do

We conduct rigorous, interdisciplinary research that examines how economic systems, policies, and practices affect the economic experience of ethnic communities. Our work includes interviews, research papers, articles, and community projects aimed towards building greater equity in the future.

Community voices

We elevate lived experiences through interviews and personal storytelling.

Cross-sector collaboration

We work with local leaders, nonprofits, and businesses.

Findings

We translate findings into meaningful stories highlight economic resilience.
