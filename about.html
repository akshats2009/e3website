About e3 Initiative

Who We Are

The e3 Initiative is a research organization committed to advancing understanding at the critical intersection of equity, economics, and ethnicity. Founded by leading scholars and practitioners, we bridge academic research with real-world policy impact.

What We Do

We conduct rigorous, interdisciplinary research that examines how economic systems, policies, and practices affect different ethnic communities. Our work provides evidence-based recommendations to policymakers, organizations, and communities working toward greater equity.
